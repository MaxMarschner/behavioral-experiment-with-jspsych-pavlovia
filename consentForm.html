Purpose of the Study. This research will enable us to better understand how people plan and produce actions when interacting with others.

Procedure. You will be asked to complete a computer task in which you will play a card game together with a virtual partner. The game involves performing simple keypresses on your key-board in response to images that appear on your computer screen. If you decide to participate, you will receive more detailed instruction about the game. The study will take about 15 minutes to complete.

Potential Risks. Any risks associated with participation in this study are equivalent to those of using a computer under normal everyday life conditions. The study does not include any unpleasant sounds or images.

Your rights as a participant. Participation in this study is voluntary. You may choose not to participate, and you may withdraw at any time during the study without giving any reason. After you have completed or withdrawn from the study, you may ask that your data be voided.

Data storage and protection. Participation in this experiment is completely anonymous, and we do not share any individual data with anyone. Your data is stored in accordance with the GDPR regulations and the website's own data protection policy.
